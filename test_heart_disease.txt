HEART DISEASE INFORMATION

Coronary artery disease (CAD) is a condition where the major blood vessels that supply the heart become damaged or diseased. The main cause is the buildup of plaque in the arteries.

Symptoms of heart disease include:
- Chest pain or discomfort
- Shortness of breath
- Fatigue
- Irregular heartbeat
- Swelling in legs, feet, or ankles

Risk factors for heart disease:
- High blood pressure
- High cholesterol
- Smoking
- Diabetes
- Obesity
- Family history
- Age
- Stress

Treatment options include:
- Lifestyle changes (diet, exercise)
- Medications (statins, blood thinners, ACE inhibitors)
- Medical procedures (angioplasty, bypass surgery)

Prevention strategies:
- Regular exercise
- Healthy diet
- No smoking
- Limit alcohol
- Manage stress
- Regular checkups
